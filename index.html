Chili Paneer

"Description:"

Welcome to my Chili Paneer recipe, it will truly make your taste buds numb, but it is truly worth it for the taste. If you like the recipe, don't forget to provide a 5 star review on my page.

"Ingredients:"

  • 2 cups of Paneer Cubed
  • 4 Green Chilli's
  • 2 inches of Ginger
  • 1 Tablespoon of Soya Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon of Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 Tablespoon of Canola Oil (for frying)

"Steps:"

  1. 1. Place the Canola Oil into a pan with medium heat for 2 minutes
  2. 2. Once the oil is ready, place the ginger and chilli into the pan, let it heat until ginger fragrance is no longer present
  3. 3. Place the Soya Sauce and Ketchup with the paneer and let it sit whilst the ginger and chilli's are frying
  4. 4. Place the paneer into the another pan and shallow fry with leftover oil on all sides until golden brown
  5. 5. Mix the Paneer with the sauce and toss for around 5 minutes to allow thorough coating
  6. 6. Serve hot and enjoy!
