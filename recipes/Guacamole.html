"Guacamole"

"Description:"

Welcome to my Guacamole recipe, it will truly make your taste buds tingle with freshness. If you like the recipe, don't forget to provide a 5 star review on my page.

"Ingredients:"

  • 4 Large Avocados
  • 2 Tablespoons of Lime Juice
  • 2 Medium Tomatoes

"Steps:"

  1. 1. Cut and Cube Avocados/li>
  2. 2. Cut and Cude Tomatoes
  3. 3. Add Lime Juice
  4. 4. Mix Well in a Medium Sized Bowl
  5. 5. Mash to desired consistency
  6. 6. Serve immediately, cover leftovers with cling film
